About BTS 방탄소년단

'21st Century Pop Icon'

BTS ( Bangtan Sonyeondan, Korean: “Bulletproof Boy Scouts” or “Bangtan Boys”), which debuted in 2013 and swept domestic and international rookie awards, has grown into the top boy group representing Korea in name and reality. BTS is causing a worldwide craze and is called a '21st century pop icon.'

It has topped the world's leading charts, including the US Billboard, UK Official Charts, Japan's Oricon, iTunes, Spotify, and Apple Music, and is setting unrivaled records in album sales, music video views, and SNS indices. In particular, BTS is the first group to conquer the top of the Billboard 'Hot 100' chart and the "Billboard 200" chart simultaneously in one week, and has topped the 'Billboard 200' five times and the 'Hot 100' four times in total. In addition, he became the first Korean singer to perform solo at the '3rd Grammy Awards', setting a record of performing on stage at three major American music award ceremonies, including the 'Billboard Music Awards', 'American Music Awards', and 'Grammy Awards'.

BTS has solidified its position as a global artist in the global concert market by holding a stadium tour, and is practicing good influence through UN speeches and the LOVE MYSELF campaign.
